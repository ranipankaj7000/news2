Farooq Abdullah steps down as National Conference chief: ‘Wont review decision’ India News

The veteran leader has served as Jammu and Kashmir's chief minister earlier.

“Dr Farooq Abdullah Sahib has informed his colleagues of his decision to step down as president of JKNC. In spite of the best efforts of senior colleagues in the party Dr Sahib was adamant that he wouldn’t review his decision,” read a party statement. “In light of this sudden announcement which has caught everyone by surprise the party General Secretary, as per the party constitution, has been tasked with conducting the election for party president which will be completed on December 5. Until that time Dr Sahib continues as President of the party. (sic)," it further added

Former Jammu and Kashmir chief minister Farooq Abdullah has stepped down as the president of the National Conference. The move comes as Jammu and Kashmir prepares for polls.
