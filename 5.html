Delhi govt think tank office sealed, LG asks CM to sack Jasmine Shah

The office of Shah was sealed on Thursday night by SDM Civil Lines following an order from the Delhi government planning department to restrict Shah and prevent his access to office

Delhi Lieutenant Governor (L-G) VK Saxena on Friday asked Delhi chief minister Arvind Kejriwal to sack Delhi government thinktank, Delhi Dialogue and Development Commission vice chairman Jasmine Shah, over charges of misusing his office for political purposes.

Meanwhile, the office of Shah was sealed on Thursday night by SDM Civil Lines following an order from the Delhi government planning department to restrict Shah and prevent his access to office.

| An official in LG office said Shah was given two opportunities to reply to the show cause notice issued on October 17 by the Director of the planning department but Shah failed to submit his reply.
